Emma J. McDonnell

About Me

I am a fourth year Ph.D. Candidate in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Dr. Leah Findlater. I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Northwestern University. My research focuses on accessible technology and I am currently interested in how technology could encourage groups to adopt more accessible communication norms.
