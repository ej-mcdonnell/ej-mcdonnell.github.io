Emma McDonnell

About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Dr. Leah Findlater. I did my bachelor's in Computer Science at Northwestern University. My research focuses on accessible technology and I am currently interested in how technology could encourage groups to adopt more accessible communication norms.
